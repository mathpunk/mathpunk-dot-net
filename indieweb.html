i believe we should own our digital identities and the data that we create for the web. some projects, organizations, and other entities promoting indie and open web principles include:
  • IndieWebCamp, the group that got me thinking about this;
  • "The open web and the opportunity of now", a talk from an alternate reality by Amber Case, which finally got me doing it;
  • ds106, a punk-as-fuck digital storytelling course, taught at University of Mary Washington but open to all;
  • Mozilla;
  • CASH Music, a nonprofit empowering musicians toward a sustainable future;
  • knight-mozilla Open News, a network of journalists, hackers, and hacker-journalists;
  • And of course, the National Security Administration, for making it all seem just a little bit more urgent than it used to.
