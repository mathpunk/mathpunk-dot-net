My mathematical interests include game theory, quantum game theory, partial orders, graph theory, combinatorics, algebra, and category theory. I am especially interested in understanding text: composing it, collaborating with it, publishing it, funding its creation, and sharing it and anti-sharing it (i.e., encryption). My current research is centered on mathematical techniques in data mining, data visualization, and design. Right now I am trying to understand the category theory of databases (see "Simplicial Databases" by Spivak), and learn the fundamentals of algebraic topology and algebraic geometry (see The Shape of Data blog).
